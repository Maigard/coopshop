Our Mission

The Atomic Cooperative Market serves the entire community as a non-profit cooperative business supplying food and other goods and services while operating in accordance with the 7 principles of the International Cooperative Association.

  • Open and voluntary membership
  • Democratic member control
  • Member economic participation
  • Autonomy and independence
  • Education, training, and information
  • Co-operation among co-operatives
  • Concern for community

We will implement these principles with the following goals:

  • Maintain the Atomic Cooperative Market as a successful and self-supporting business
  • Enable the community to choose their food products and suppliers based on their own needs, concerns, and priorities.
  • Enhance access to wholesome, healthy, and natural food to the broad community regardless of background, culture, or economic resources.
  • Support the local economy prioritizing values and principles over monetary concerns.
  • Operate with concern for social, economic, and environmental sustainability
  • Operate with concern for social, economic, and environmental justice
  • Operate with transparency and trust by giving the community full information (and therefore influence) about the business choices and practices of the co-op..
  • Educate the community about matters that affect its own health and sustainability and that of the Earth and it's inhabitants
